Thomas T. Daniel holds a Ph.D. in Nanotechnology from IIT Guwahati, specializing in device fabrication for sensing applications. He is the co-founder of K-FAB Tech Private Limited, a company dedicated to making semiconductor fabrication equipment accessible to researchers. Thomas has also worked at the India Innovation Centre for Graphene (IICG) as a project manager for the cleanroom, providing consultancy to various research and academic institutions on the setup of advanced micro and nanofabrication facilities.
