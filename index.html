What We Saw!

Our initiatives and collaborations have driven significant advancements across various fields. We've enabled the production of submicron patterns in semiconductor research without high vacuum and temperature processes. Our facilities have supported the development of advanced agricultural sensors, life-saving medical devices, and automotive semiconductor components that enhance safety and performance. We've fostered a collaborative, interdisciplinary community, promoting sustainability through low-cost, efficient fabrication methods. These efforts underscore the power of accessibility and collaboration in driving technological progress and delivering real-world impacts.



What We Did!

We democratized semiconductor fabrication with affordable, advanced facilities. We deployed cutting-edge patterning and electrical characterization technology, and developed a low-cost system for submicron patterns without high vacuum and temperature processes. We introduced modular cleanrooms for optimal research conditions and supported innovation in agriculture, medicine, and automotive sectors. Our collaborative community of researchers, engineers, and scientists promotes sustainable and efficient semiconductor fabrication.



Our Product

Our products support diverse industry applications. We offer a low-cost semiconductor fabrication system for precise submicron patterns without high vacuum and temperature processes, integrated with electrical characterization. Our facilities feature state-of-the-art patterning, high-precision instruments, and sensing chambers. We provide customizable cleanrooms for contaminant-free research, along with training, collaborative projects, and technical support. Our products enhance precision farming, medical device development, and automotive safety and performance.
