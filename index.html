What We Saw!

Conventional methods pose formidable challenges, requiring sophisticated fabrication techniques, top-tier equipment, and pricey state-of-the-art labs for deposition, patterning, and etching.

What We Did!

We confront the limitations of traditional electronic device fabrication head-on by introducing an alternative technique. With our method, we offer unparalleled precision and resolution without the need for masks, vacuum chambers, or high temperatures. By eliminating these constraints, we enable much closer micropatterning, revolutionizing the landscape of electronic fabrication.

Our Product

Revolutionizing semiconductor fabrication, our integrated system merges affordability with advanced functionality. Sidestepping the need for high vacuum or temperature, it pioneers submicron patterning on diverse substrates. With versatile ink capabilities, it empowers the seamless production of resistor diodes and transistors, setting new standards in efficiency and precision.
